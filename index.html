Recipe 1: Luke's Diner Special

  1. Brew 2 espresso shots.
  2. Add sugar and cream to taste.
  3. Pour it into a big mug.
  4. Enjoy while gossiping with Lorelai and Rory.

Recipe 2: Lorelai's Pumpkin Latte

  1. Brew 1 espresso shot.
  2. Steam milk and mix with pumpkin puree.
  3. Heat sugar to make a simple syrup.
  4. Combine all the ingredients.
